History




Hogwarts is a magical school in England. It was built by four great wizards of

Gordic Griffin, Salazar Slytherin, Helga Hupphof, and Rowena Rabanne. Each of

the four dorms is named after their name. They built schools in Hogwarts castle.

Slytherin, however, hated students who had Muggle parents because they thought

magic should be taught only to pure-blood sorcerers, all of whom were wizards.

Because of this, there was a conflict between Gryffindor and Slytherin, and

Slytherin eventually left school. Then he secretly built a secret room in Hogwarts.

That is the secret room, which is also the title of Harry Potter 2. Slytherin

blocked the secret room to prevent anyone from opening until his successor was admitted

to school. Only the successor is to open the secret room and release the terrible things

in it, to remove the students who are not worthy of studying the magic. When you enter

Hogwart, the magic hat first assigns the dorm to the students. Hogwart lives in a
