Admission Condition




1. Children of British nationality.

2. 11-year-olds who have received a letter of admission.









Preparations for Admission


School Uniform



1. Patternless long coat (black)

2. One pointed hat (black)

3. Protective gloves (leather, etc.)

4. Winter cloak (black silver button)

※ All clothes must have a name tag ※









Textbook



1. Standard Spellbook (1st Grade)

2. History of Magic

3. Magic Theory

4. Guide for beginners

5. 1000 kinds of magic herbs and mold

6. Magic and magic medicine

7. Occult Animal Dictionary

8. Power of Darkness: Defense Directive








Other Consumables



1. A magic trick

2. Large pot

3. Glass or crystal bottle

4. One telescope

5. Brass Scale One

※ You can bring pets such as owls, cats, toads, and mice. ※








Directions



1. Pass through the pillar between Kings Cross Station 9 and 10.

2. Take the express train on the 9 and 3/4 platform.

3. Take a boat along Hagrid to Hogwart School of Magic.
